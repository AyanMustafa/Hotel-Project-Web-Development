About Us

In 1945, inspired by their unique bond and shared dreams, Ayan, Humphrey, Selina, and Treasure Fantastico founded **Paradise Lagoon**. Their vision was to create a retreat that embodied the essence of luxury, infused with the warmth of family heritage. Today, Paradise Lagoon stands as a testament to their passion, offering guests an enchanting escape into elegance and history.
